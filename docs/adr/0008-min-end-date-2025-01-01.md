# ADR-0008: Cap market fetch window at 2025-01-01

**Date:** 2026-05-20
**Status:** Accepted

## Context

The pipeline fetches resolved political markets from the Polymarket Gamma API filtered by `MIN_END_DATE`. Lowering this to `2024-01-01` pulls in large 2024 election markets — notably the US presidential election (~$1B volume) and Fed rate decisions — which have enormous trade tables on Dune. The Dune wallet query (`polymarket_polygon.market_trades`) scans the full table and its resource cost scales with total trade volume across all markets in the query. The per-query resource cap on the free Dune tier is hit when these markets are included, causing the wallet query to fail.

## Decision

Keep `MIN_END_DATE="2025-01-01"` in `backend/config.py`, limiting the pipeline to markets that resolved in 2025 or later.

## Alternatives Considered

**`MIN_END_DATE="2024-01-01"`** — Tried briefly to include more labeled cases. Caused Dune wallet query failures (`QUERY_STATE_FAILED: RESOURCES_CAP_REACHED`) because 2024 election markets have 10–100× more trades than typical 2025 markets. Reverted.

**Batching the Dune query** — Run the wallet query in chunks of N markets instead of all at once. Would work but adds complexity and multiple round trips. Deferred: the date cap is simpler and sufficient for now.

**Filtering out high-volume markets from the wallet query** — Cap wallet queries at markets below some volume threshold (e.g. $100M). Analytically defensible (billion-dollar markets are unlikely insider trading candidates) but adds a new parameter. Not implemented yet.

## Consequences

- The 2 Kalshi-only labeled cases (`kalshi_mrbeast`, `kalshi_langford`) that predate 2025 remain outside the wallet query window anyway (they're Kalshi, not Polymarket). No loss there.
- Any new labeled cases with `end_date < 2025-01-01` will get price features but no wallet features.
- If the Dune resource cap is raised (paid plan, or MCP-based query routing), revisiting `2024-07-01` or earlier is reasonable — the 2024 H2 markets are more interesting than 2024 H1.
